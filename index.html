How I work

Design Process

From start to finish my goal is to create thoughtful design solutions that create enjoyable experiences, click or tap on each phase of the design thinking process to learn more.

Research

At the beginning of every project, I make it a point to sit down with all stakeholders to ensure that we're all on the same page and asking the right questions. Our goal is to develop an empathic understanding of the problem we are trying to solve by learning about the user, the company, and the competition.

Definition & Ideation

By this point in the design thinking process, I've gathered all my findings from the research phase and will analyze and synthesize research to define the core problem. I will proceed to brainstorm solutions, and by the end of this phase, I will have defined a value proposition.

Prototyping & testing

At this point, I'll start mapping out user flows, sketching out wireframes, and I'll produce a minimum viable product (MVP) based on ideas identified in the previous phase. I'll then proceed to test the design and redesign based on user feedback. At the end of this stage, I'll have a proven, tested design ready for development.
